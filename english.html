About Me

I am Milica Brborović Morača, an ambitious and creative web designer with three years of experience. My field of work is frontend design, which includes work in Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, Boostrap, and jQuery. When I change photos and make some banners, I use Photoshop.
Also, I'm working with some backend technologies such are PHP program languages and MySQL database. Besides, I'm operating with WordPress and use all its advantages.
One of my activities is content writing for and editing articles for the WebFactory platformu, about web design, WordPress, digital marketing, etc.
Also, I deal with digital marketing, which includes banner designing, writing content for social networks, creating ads for Google and Facebook, and using tools for making email campaigns.
Teaching is my particular passion. Due to that, I work as a lecturer of web design in InfoArs Academy. I am looking forward to getting in touch with you. Therefore feel free to ask me anything you want to know.
